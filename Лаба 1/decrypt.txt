CSAPTER 1

A LONW-EQPECTED PARTG

BSEN YR. FILFO FAWWINH OM FAW END ANNOUNCED TSAT SE BOULD HSORTLG FE CELEFRATINW SIH ELEKENTG-MIRHT FIRTSDAG BITS A PARTG OM HPECIAL YAWNIMICENCE, TSERE BAH YUCS TALV AND EQCITEYENT IN SOFFITON.
FILFO BAH KERG RICS AND KERG PECULIAR, AND SAD FEEN TSE BONDER OM TSE HSIRE MOR HIQTG GEARH, EKER HINCE SIH REYARVAFLE DIHAPPEARANCE AND UNEQPECTED RETURN. TSE RICSEH SE SAD FROUWST FACV MROY SIH TRAKELH SAD NOB FECOYE A LOCAL LEWEND, AND IT BAH POPULARLG FELIEKED, BSATEKER TSE OLD MOLV YIWST HAG, TSAT TSE SILL AT FAW END BAH MULL OM TUNNELH HTUMMED BITS TREAHURE. AND IM TSAT BAH NOT ENOUWS MOR MAYE, TSERE BAH ALHO SIH PROLONWED KIWOUR TO YARKEL AT. TIYE BORE ON, FUT IT HEEYED TO SAKE LITTLE EMMECT ON YR. FAWWINH. AT NINETG SE BAH YUCS TSE HAYE AH AT MIMTG. AT NINETG-NINE TSEG FEWAN TO CALL SIY BELL-PREHERKED, FUT UNCSANWED BOULD SAKE FEEN NEARER TSE YARV. TSERE BERE HOYE TSAT HSOOV TSEIR SEADH AND TSOUWST TSIH BAH TOO YUCS OM A WOOD TSINW; IT HEEYED UNMAIR TSAT ANGONE HSOULD POHHEHH (APPARENTLG) PERPETUAL GOUTS AH BELL AH (REPUTEDLG) INEQSAUHTIFLE BEALTS.
‘IT BILL SAKE TO FE PAID MOR,’ TSEG HAID. ‘IT IHN’T NATURAL, AND TROUFLE BILL COYE OM IT!’
FUT HO MAR TROUFLE SAD NOT COYE; AND AH YR. FAWWINH BAH WENEROUH BITS SIH YONEG, YOHT PEOPLE BERE BILLINW TO MORWIKE SIY SIH ODDITIEH AND SIH WOOD MORTUNE. SE REYAINED ON KIHITINW TERYH BITS SIH RELATIKEH (EQCEPT, OM COURHE, TSE HACVKILLE-FAWWINHEH), AND SE SAD YANG DEKOTED ADYIRERH AYONW TSE SOFFITH OM POOR AND UNIYPORTANT MAYILIEH. FUT SE SAD NO CLOHE MRIENDH, UNTIL HOYE OM SIH GOUNWER COUHINH FEWAN TO WROB UP.
TSE ELDEHT OM TSEHE, AND FILFO’H MAKOURITE, BAH GOUNW MRODO FAWWINH. BSEN FILFO BAH NINETG-NINE, SE ADOPTED MRODO AH SIH SEIR, AND FROUWST SIY TO LIKE AT FAW END; AND TSE SOPEH OM TSE HACVKILLE-FAWWINHEH BERE MINALLG DAHSED. FILFO AND MRODO SAPPENED TO SAKE TSE HAYE FIRTSDAG, HEPTEYFER 22ND. ‘GOU SAD FETTER COYE AND LIKE SERE, MRODO YG LAD,’ HAID FILFO ONE DAG; ‘AND TSEN BE CAN CELEFRATE OUR FIRTSDAG-PARTIEH COYMORTAFLG TOWETSER.’ AT TSAT TIYE MRODO BAH HTILL IN SIH TBEENH, AH TSE SOFFITH CALLED TSE IRREHPONHIFLE TBENTIEH FETBEEN CSILDSOOD AND COYINW OM AWE AT TSIRTG-TSREE.
TBELKE YORE GEARH PAHHED. EACS GEAR TSE FAWWINHEH SAD WIKEN KERG LIKELG COYFINED FIRTSDAG-PARTIEH AT FAW END; FUT NOB IT BAH UNDERHTOOD TSAT HOYETSINW JUITE EQCEPTIONAL BAH FEINW PLANNED MOR TSAT AUTUYN. FILFO BAH WOINW TO FE ELEKENTG-ONE, 111, A RATSER CURIOUH NUYFER AND A KERG REHPECTAFLE AWE MOR A SOFFIT (TSE OLD TOOV SIYHELM SAD ONLG REACSED 130); AND MRODO BAH WOINW TO FE TSIRTG-TSREE, 33) AN IYPORTANT NUYFER: TSE DATE OM SIH ‘COYINW OM AWE’.
TONWUEH FEWAN TO BAW IN SOFFITON AND FGBATER; AND RUYOUR OM TSE COYINW EKENT TRAKELLED ALL OKER TSE HSIRE. TSE SIHTORG AND CSARACTER OM YR. FILFO FAWWINH FECAYE ONCE AWAIN TSE CSIEM TOPIC OM CONKERHATION; AND TSE OLDER MOLV HUDDENLG MOUND TSEIR REYINIHCENCEH IN BELCOYE DEYAND.
NO ONE SAD A YORE ATTENTIKE AUDIENCE TSAN OLD SAY WAYWEE, COYYONLG VNOBN AH TSE WAMMER. SE SELD MORTS AT TSE IKG FUHS, A HYALL INN ON TSE FGBATER ROAD; AND SE HPOVE BITS HOYE AUTSORITG, MOR SE SAD TENDED TSE WARDEN AT FAW END MOR MORTG GEARH, AND SAD SELPED OLD SOLYAN IN TSE HAYE XOF FEMORE TSAT. NOB TSAT SE BAH SIYHELM WROBINW OLD AND HTIMM IN TSE XOINTH, TSE XOF BAH YAINLG CARRIED ON FG SIH GOUNWEHT HON, HAY WAYWEE. FOTS MATSER AND HON BERE ON KERG MRIENDLG TERYH BITS FILFO AND MRODO. TSEG LIKED ON TSE SILL ITHELM, IN NUYFER 3 FAWHSOT ROB XUHT FELOB FAW END.
‘A KERG NICE BELL-HPOVEN WENTLESOFFIT IH YR. FILFO, AH I’KE ALBAGH HAID,’ TSE WAMMER DECLARED. BITS PERMECT TRUTS: MOR FILFO BAH KERG POLITE TO SIY, CALLINW SIY ‘YAHTER SAYMAHT’, AND CONHULTINW SIY CONHTANTLG UPON TSE WROBINW OM KEWETAFLEH - IN TSE YATTER OM ‘ROOTH’, EHPECIALLG POTATOEH, TSE WAMMER BAH RECOWNIZED AH TSE LEADINW AUTSORITG FG ALL IN TSE NEIWSFOURSOOD (INCLUDINW SIYHELM).
‘FUT BSAT AFOUT TSIH MRODO TSAT LIKEH BITS SIY?’ AHVED OLD NOAVEH OM FGBATER. ‘FAWWINH IH SIH NAYE, FUT SE’H YORE TSAN SALM A FRANDGFUCV, TSEG HAG. IT FEATH YE BSG ANG FAWWINH OM SOFFITON HSOULD WO LOOVINW MOR A BIME ABAG TSERE IN FUCVLAND, BSERE MOLVH ARE HO JUEER.’
‘AND NO BONDER TSEG’RE JUEER,’ PUT IN DADDG TBOMOOT (TSE WAMMER’H NEQT-DOOR NEIWSFOUR), ‘IM TSEG LIKE ON TSE BRONW HIDE OM TSE FRANDGBINE RIKER, AND RIWST AWIN TSE OLD MOREHT. TSAT’H A DARV FAD PLACE, IM SALM TSE TALEH FE TRUE.’
‘GOU’RE RIWST, DAD!’ HAID TSE WAMMER. ‘NOT TSAT TSE FRANDGFUCVH OM FUCV-LAND LIKE IN TSE OLD MOREHT; FUT TSEG’RE A JUEER FREED, HEEYINWLG. TSEG MOOL AFOUT BITS FOATH ON TSAT FIW RIKER - AND TSAT IHN’T NATURAL. HYALL BONDER TSAT TROUFLE CAYE OM IT, I HAG. FUT FE TSAT AH IT YAG, YR. MRODO IH AH NICE A GOUNW SOFFIT AH GOU COULD BIHS TO YEET. KERG YUCS LIVE YR. FILFO, AND IN YORE TSAN LOOVH. AMTER ALL SIH MATSER BAH A FAWWINH. A DECENT REHPECTAFLE SOFFIT BAH YR. DROWO FAWWINH; TSERE BAH NEKER YUCS TO TELL OM SIY, TILL SE BAH DROBNDED.’
‘DROBNDED?’ HAID HEKERAL KOICEH. TSEG SAD SEARD TSIH AND OTSER DARVER RUYOURH FEMORE, OM COURHE; FUT SOFFITH SAKE A PAHHION MOR MAYILG SIHTORG, AND TSEG BERE READG TO SEAR IT AWAIN. ‘BELL, HO TSEG HAG,’ HAID TSE WAMMER. ‘GOU HEE: YR. DROWO, SE YARRIED POOR YIHH PRIYULA FRANDGFUCV. HSE BAH OUR YR. FILFO’H MIRHT COUHIN ON TSE YOTSER’H HIDE (SER YOTSER FEINW TSE GOUNWEHT OM TSE OLD TOOV’H DAUWSTERH); AND YR. DROWO BAH SIH HECOND COUHIN. HO YR. MRODO IH SIH MIRHT AND HECOND COUHIN, ONCE REYOKED EITSER BAG, AH TSE HAGINW IH, IM GOU MOLLOB YE. AND YR. DROWO BAH HTAGINW AT FRANDG SALL BITS SIH MATSER-IN-LAB, OLD YAHTER WORFADOC, AH SE OMTEN DID AMTER SIH YARRIAWE (SIY FEINW PARTIAL TO SIH KITTLEH, AND OLD WORFADOC VEEPINW A YIWSTG WENEROUH TAFLE); AND SE BENT OUT FOATINW ON TSE FRANDGBINE RIKER; AND SE AND SIH BIME BERE DROBNDED, AND POOR YR. MRODO ONLG A CSILD AND ALL. ‘
‘I’KE SEARD TSEG BENT ON TSE BATER AMTER DINNER IN TSE YOONLIWST,’ HAID OLD NOAVEH; ‘AND IT BAH DROWO’H BEIWST AH HUNV TSE FOAT.’
‘AND I SEARD HSE PUHSED SIY IN, AND SE PULLED SER IN AMTER SIY,’ HAID HANDGYAN, TSE SOFFITON YILLER.
‘GOU HSOULDN’T LIHTEN TO ALL GOU SEAR, HANDGYAN,’ HAID TSE WAMMER, BSO DID NOT YUCS LIVE TSE YILLER. ‘TSERE IHN’T NO CALL TO WO TALVINW OM PUHSINW AND PULLINW. FOATH ARE JUITE TRICVG ENOUWS MOR TSOHE TSAT HIT HTILL BITSOUT LOOVINW MURTSER MOR TSE CAUHE OM TROUFLE. ANGBAG: TSERE BAH TSIH YR. MRODO LEMT AN ORPSAN AND HTRANDED, AH GOU YIWST HAG, AYONW TSOHE JUEER FUCVLANDERH, FEINW FROUWST UP ANGSOB IN FRANDG SALL. A REWULAR BARREN, FG ALL ACCOUNTH. OLD YAHTER WORFADOC NEKER SAD MEBER TSAN A COUPLE OM SUNDRED RELATIONH IN TSE PLACE. YR. FILFO NEKER DID A VINDER DEED TSAN BSEN SE FROUWST TSE LAD FACV TO LIKE AYONW DECENT MOLV.
‘FUT I RECVON IT BAH A NAHTG HSOCV MOR TSOHE HACVKILLE-FAWWINHEH. TSEG TSOUWST TSEG BERE WOINW TO WET FAW END, TSAT TIYE BSEN SE BENT OMM AND BAH TSOUWST TO FE DEAD. AND TSEN SE COYEH FACV AND ORDERH TSEY OMM; AND SE WOEH ON LIKINW AND LIKINW, AND NEKER LOOVINW A DAG OLDER, FLEHH SIY! AND HUDDENLG SE PRODUCEH AN SEIR, AND SAH ALL TSE PAPERH YADE OUT PROPER. TSE HACVKILLE-FAWWINHEH BON’T NEKER HEE TSE INHIDE OM FAW END NOB, OR IT IH TO FE SOPED NOT.’
‘TSERE’H A TIDG FIT OM YONEG TUCVED ABAG UP TSERE, I SEAR TELL,’ HAID A HTRANWER, A KIHITOR ON FUHINEHH MROY YICSEL DELKINW IN TSE BEHTMARTSINW. ‘ALL TSE TOP OM GOUR SILL IH MULL OM TUNNELH PACVED BITS CSEHTH OM WOLD AND HILKER, AND XOOLH, FG BSAT I’KE SEARD. ‘
‘TSEN GOU’KE SEARD YORE TSAN I CAN HPEAV TO,’ ANHBERED TSE WAMMER. I VNOB NOTSINW AFOUT XOOLH. YR. FILFO IH MREE BITS SIH YONEG, AND TSERE HEEYH NO LACV OM IT; FUT I VNOB OM NO TUNNEL-YAVINW. I HAB YR. FILFO BSEN SE CAYE FACV, A YATTER OM HIQTG GEARH AWO, BSEN I BAH A LAD. I’D NOT LONW COYE PRENTICE TO OLD SOLYAN (SIY FEINW YG DAD’H COUHIN), FUT SE SAD YE UP AT FAW END SELPINW SIY TO VEEP MOLVH MROY TRAYPLINW AND TRAPEHHINW ALL OKER TSE WARDEN BSILE TSE HALE BAH ON. AND IN TSE YIDDLE OM IT ALL YR. FILFO COYEH UP TSE SILL BITS A PONG AND HOYE YIWSTG FIW FAWH AND A COUPLE OM CSEHTH. I DON’T DOUFT TSEG BERE YOHTLG MULL OM TREAHURE SE SAD PICVED UP IN MOREIWN PARTH, BSERE TSERE FE YOUNTAINH OM WOLD, TSEG HAG; FUT TSERE BAHN’T ENOUWS TO MILL TUNNELH. FUT YG LAD HAY BILL VNOB YORE AFOUT TSAT. SE’H IN AND OUT OM FAW END. CRAZG AFOUT HTORIEH OM TSE OLD DAGH SE IH, AND SE LIHTENH TO ALL YR. FILFO’H TALEH. YR. FILFO SAH LEARNED SIY SIH LETTERH - YEANINW NO SARY, YARV GOU, AND I SOPE NO SARY BILL COYE OM IT.
‘ELKEH AND DRAWONH’ I HAGH TO SIY. ‘CAFFAWEH AND POTATOEH ARE FETTER MOR YE AND GOU. DON’T WO WETTINW YIQED UP IN TSE FUHINEHH OM GOUR FETTERH, OR GOU’LL LAND IN TROUFLE TOO FIW MOR GOU,’ I HAGH TO SIY. AND I YIWST HAG IT TO OTSERH,’ SE ADDED BITS A LOOV AT TSE HTRANWER AND TSE YILLER.
FUT TSE WAMMER DID NOT CONKINCE SIH AUDIENCE. TSE LEWEND OM FILFO’H BEALTS BAH NOB TOO MIRYLG MIQED IN TSE YINDH OM TSE GOUNWER WENERATION OM SOFFITH.
‘AS, FUT SE SAH LIVELG ENOUWS FEEN ADDINW TO BSAT SE FROUWST AT MIRHT,’ ARWUED TSE YILLER, KOICINW COYYON OPINION. ‘SE’H OMTEN ABAG MROY SOYE. AND LOOV AT TSE OUTLANDIHS MOLV TSAT KIHIT SIY: DBARKEH COYINW AT NIWST, AND TSAT OLD BANDERINW CONXUROR, WANDALM, AND ALL. GOU CAN HAG BSAT GOU LIVE, WAMMER, FUT FAW END’H A JUEER PLACE, AND ITH MOLV ARE JUEERER.’
‘AND GOU CAN HAG BSAT GOU LIVE, AFOUT BSAT GOU VNOB NO YORE OM TSAN GOU DO OM FOATINW, YR. HANDGYAN,’ RETORTED TSE WAMMER, DIHLIVINW TSE YILLER EKEN YORE TSAN UHUAL. IM TSAT’H FEINW JUEER, TSEN BE COULD DO BITS A FIT YORE JUEERNEHH IN TSEHE PARTH. TSERE’H HOYE NOT MAR ABAG TSAT BOULDN’T OMMER A PINT OM FEER TO A MRIEND, IM TSEG LIKED IN A SOLE BITS WOLDEN BALLH. FUT TSEG DO TSINWH PROPER AT FAW END. OUR HAY HAGH TSAT EKERGONE’H WOINW TO FE INKITED TO TSE PARTG, AND TSERE’H WOINW TO FE PREHENTH, YARV GOU, PREHENTH MOR ALL - TSIH KERG YONTS AH IH.’
TSAT KERG YONTS BAH HEPTEYFER, AND AH MINE AH GOU COULD AHV. A DAG OR TBO LATER A RUYOUR (PROFAFLG HTARTED FG TSE VNOBLEDWEAFLE HAY) BAH HPREAD AFOUT TSAT TSERE BERE WOINW TO FE MIREBORVH - MIREBORVH, BSAT IH YORE, HUCS AH SAD NOT FEEN HEEN IN TSE HSIRE MOR NIWS ON A CENTURG, NOT INDEED HINCE TSE OLD TOOV DIED.
DAGH PAHHED AND TSE DAG DREB NEARER. AN ODD-LOOVINW BAWWON LADEN BITS ODD-LOOVINW PACVAWEH ROLLED INTO SOFFITON ONE EKENINW AND TOILED UP TSE SILL TO FAW END. TSE HTARTLED SOFFITH PEERED OUT OM LAYPLIT DOORH TO WAPE AT IT. IT BAH DRIKEN FG OUTLANDIHS MOLV, HINWINW HTRANWE HONWH: DBARKEH BITS LONW FEARDH AND DEEP SOODH. A MEB OM TSEY REYAINED AT FAW END. AT TSE END OM TSE HECOND BEEV IN HEPTEYFER A CART CAYE IN TSROUWS FGBATER MROY TSE DIRECTION OM TSE FRANDGBINE FRIDWE IN FROAD DAGLIWST. AN OLD YAN BAH DRIKINW IT ALL ALONE. SE BORE A TALL POINTED FLUE SAT, A LONW WREG CLOAV, AND A HILKER HCARM. SE SAD A LONW BSITE FEARD AND FUHSG EGEFROBH TSAT HTUCV OUT FEGOND TSE FRIY OM SIH SAT. HYALL SOFFIT-CSILDREN RAN AMTER TSE CART ALL TSROUWS SOFFITON AND RIWST UP TSE SILL. IT SAD A CARWO OM MIREBORVH, AH TSEG RIWSTLG WUEHHED. AT FILFO’H MRONT DOOR TSE OLD YAN FEWAN TO UNLOAD: TSERE BERE WREAT FUNDLEH OM MIREBORVH OM ALL HORTH AND HSAPEH, EACS LAFELLED BITS A LARWE RED W AND TSE ELM-RUNE, .
TSAT BAH WANDALM’H YARV, OM COURHE, AND TSE OLD YAN BAH WANDALM TSE BIZARD, BSOHE MAYE IN TSE HSIRE BAH DUE YAINLG TO SIH HVILL BITS MIREH, HYOVEH, AND LIWSTH. SIH REAL FUHINEHH BAH MAR YORE DIMMICULT AND DANWEROUH, FUT TSE HSIRE-MOLV VNEB NOTSINW AFOUT IT. TO TSEY SE BAH XUHT ONE OM TSE ‘ATTRACTIONH’ AT TSE PARTG. SENCE TSE EQCITEYENT OM TSE SOFFIT-CSILDREN. ‘W MOR WRAND!’ TSEG HSOUTED, AND TSE OLD YAN HYILED. TSEG VNEB SIY FG HIWST, TSOUWS SE ONLG APPEARED IN SOFFITON OCCAHIONALLG AND NEKER HTOPPED LONW; FUT NEITSER TSEG NOR ANG FUT TSE OLDEHT OM TSEIR ELDERH SAD HEEN ONE OM SIH MIREBORV DIHPLAGH - TSEG NOB FELONWED TO TSE LEWENDARG PAHT.
BSEN TSE OLD YAN, SELPED FG FILFO AND HOYE DBARKEH, SAD MINIHSED UNLOADINW. FILFO WAKE A MEB PENNIEH ABAG; FUT NOT A HINWLE HJUIF OR CRACVER BAH MORTSCOYINW, TO TSE DIHAPPOINTYENT OM TSE ONLOOVERH.
‘RUN ABAG NOB!’ HAID WANDALM. ‘GOU BILL WET PLENTG BSEN TSE TIYE COYEH.’ TSEN SE DIHAPPEARED INHIDE BITS FILFO, AND TSE DOOR BAH HSUT. TSE GOUNW SOFFITH HTARED AT TSE DOOR IN KAIN MOR A BSILE, AND TSEN YADE OMM, MEELINW TSAT TSE DAG OM TSE PARTG BOULD NEKER COYE.
INHIDE FAW END, FILFO AND WANDALM BERE HITTINW AT TSE OPEN BINDOB OM A HYALL ROOY LOOVINW OUT BEHT ON TO TSE WARDEN. TSE LATE AMTERNOON BAH FRIWST AND PEACEMUL. TSE MLOBERH WLOBED RED AND WOLDEN: HNAP-DRAWONH AND HUN-MLOBERH, AND NAHTURTIUYH TRAILINW ALL OKER TSE TURM BALLH AND PEEPINW IN AT TSE ROUND BINDOBH.
‘SOB FRIWST GOUR WARDEN LOOVH!’ HAID WANDALM.
‘GEH,’ HAID FILFO. I AY KERG MOND INDEED OM IT, AND OM ALL TSE DEAR OLD HSIRE; FUT I TSINV I NEED A SOLIDAG.’
‘GOU YEAN TO WO ON BITS GOUR PLAN TSEN?’
‘I DO. I YADE UP YG YIND YONTSH AWO, AND I SAKEN’T CSANWED IT.’
‘KERG BELL. IT IH NO WOOD HAGINW ANG YORE. HTICV TO GOUR PLAN - GOUR BSOLE PLAN, YIND - AND I SOPE IT BILL TURN OUT MOR TSE FEHT, MOR GOU, AND MOR ALL OM UH.’
‘I SOPE HO. ANGBAG I YEAN TO ENXOG YGHELM ON TSURHDAG, AND SAKE YG LITTLE XOVE.’
‘BSO BILL LAUWS, I BONDER?’ HAID WANDALM, HSAVINW SIH SEAD.
‘BE HSALL HEE,’ HAID FILFO.
TSE NEQT DAG YORE CARTH ROLLED UP TSE SILL, AND HTILL YORE CARTH. TSERE YIWST SAKE FEEN HOYE WRUYFLINW AFOUT ‘DEALINW LOCALLG’, FUT TSAT KERG BEEV ORDERH FEWAN TO POUR OUT OM FAW END MOR EKERG VIND OM PROKIHION, COYYODITG, OR LUQURG TSAT COULD FE OFTAINED IN SOFFITON OR FGBATER OR ANGBSERE IN TSE NEIWSFOURSOOD. PEOPLE FECAYE ENTSUHIAHTIC; AND TSEG FEWAN TO TICV OMM TSE DAGH ON TSE CALENDAR; AND TSEG BATCSED EAWERLG MOR TSE POHTYAN, SOPINW MOR INKITATIONH.
FEMORE LONW TSE INKITATIONH FEWAN POURINW OUT, AND TSE SOFFITON POHT-OMMICE BAH FLOCVED, AND TSE FGBATER POHT-OMMICE BAH HNOBED UNDER, AND KOLUNTARG AHHIHTANT POHTYEN BERE CALLED MOR. TSERE BAH A CONHTANT HTREAY OM TSEY WOINW UP TSE SILL, CARRGINW SUNDREDH OM POLITE KARIATIONH ON TSANV GOU, I HSALL CERTAINLG COYE.
A NOTICE APPEARED ON TSE WATE AT FAW END: NO ADYITTANCE EQCEPT ON PARTG FUHINEHH. EKEN TSOHE BSO SAD, OR PRETENDED TO SAKE PARTG FUHINEHH BERE HELDOY ALLOBED INHIDE. FILFO BAH FUHG: BRITINW INKITATIONH, TICVINW OMM ANHBERH, PACVINW UP PREHENTH, AND YAVINW HOYE PRIKATE PREPARATIONH OM SIH OBN. MROY TSE TIYE OM WANDALM’H ARRIKAL SE REYAINED SIDDEN MROY KIEB.
ONE YORNINW TSE SOFFITH BOVE TO MIND TSE LARWE MIELD, HOUTS OM FILFO’H MRONT DOOR, COKERED BITS ROPEH AND POLEH MOR TENTH AND PAKILIONH. A HPECIAL ENTRANCE BAH CUT INTO TSE FANV LEADINW TO TSE ROAD, AND BIDE HTEPH AND A LARWE BSITE WATE BERE FUILT TSERE. TSE TSREE SOFFIT-MAYILIEH OM FAWHSOT ROB, ADXOININW TSE MIELD, BERE INTENHELG INTEREHTED AND WENERALLG ENKIED. OLD WAMMER WAYWEE HTOPPED EKEN PRETENDINW TO BORV IN SIH WARDEN.
TSE TENTH FEWAN TO WO UP. TSERE BAH A HPECIALLG LARWE PAKILION, HO FIW TSAT TSE TREE TSAT WREB IN TSE MIELD BAH RIWST INHIDE IT, AND HTOOD PROUDLG NEAR ONE END, AT TSE SEAD OM TSE CSIEM TAFLE. LANTERNH BERE SUNW ON ALL ITH FRANCSEH. YORE PROYIHINW HTILL (TO TSE SOFFITH’ YIND): AN ENORYOUH OPEN-AIR VITCSEN BAH ERECTED IN TSE NORTS CORNER OM TSE MIELD. A DRAUWST OM COOVH, MROY EKERG INN AND EATINW-SOUHE MOR YILEH AROUND, ARRIKED TO HUPPLEYENT TSE DBARKEH AND OTSER ODD MOLV TSAT BERE JUARTERED AT FAW END. EQCITEYENT ROHE TO ITH SEIWST.
TSEN TSE BEATSER CLOUDED OKER. TSAT BAH ON BEDNEHDAG TSE EKE OM TSE PARTG. ANQIETG BAH INTENHE. TSEN TSURHDAG, HEPTEYFER TSE 22ND, ACTUALLG DABNED. TSE HUN WOT UP, TSE CLOUDH KANIHSED, MLAWH BERE UNMURLED AND TSE MUN FEWAN.
FILFO FAWWINH CALLED IT A PARTG, FUT IT BAH REALLG A KARIETG OM ENTERTAINYENTH ROLLED INTO ONE. PRACTICALLG EKERGFODG LIKINW NEAR BAH INKITED. A KERG MEB BERE OKERLOOVED FG ACCIDENT, FUT AH TSEG TURNED UP ALL TSE HAYE, TSAT DID NOT YATTER. YANG PEOPLE MROY OTSER PARTH OM TSE HSIRE BERE ALHO AHVED; AND TSERE BERE EKEN A MEB MROY OUTHIDE TSE FORDERH. FILFO YET TSE WUEHTH (AND ADDITIONH) AT TSE NEB BSITE WATE IN PERHON. SE WAKE ABAG PREHENTH TO ALL AND HUNDRG - TSE LATTER BERE TSOHE BSO BENT OUT AWAIN FG A FACV BAG AND CAYE IN AWAIN FG TSE WATE. SOFFITH WIKE PREHENTH TO OTSER PEOPLE ON TSEIR OBN FIRTSDAGH. NOT KERG EQPENHIKE ONEH, AH A RULE, AND NOT HO LAKIHSLG AH ON TSIH OCCAHION; FUT IT BAH NOT A FAD HGHTEY. ACTUALLG IN SOFFITON AND FGBATER EKERG DAG IN TSE GEAR IT BAH HOYEFODG’H FIRTSDAG, HO TSAT EKERG SOFFIT IN TSOHE PARTH SAD A MAIR CSANCE OM AT LEAHT ONE PREHENT AT LEAHT ONCE A BEEV. FUT TSEG NEKER WOT TIRED OM TSEY.
ON TSIH OCCAHION TSE PREHENTH BERE UNUHUALLG WOOD. TSE SOFFIT-CSILDREN BERE HO EQCITED TSAT MOR A BSILE TSEG ALYOHT MORWOT AFOUT EATINW. TSERE BERE TOGH TSE LIVE OM BSICS TSEG SAD NEKER HEEN FEMORE, ALL FEAUTIMUL AND HOYE OFKIOUHLG YAWICAL. YANG OM TSEY SAD INDEED FEEN ORDERED A GEAR FEMORE, AND SAD COYE ALL TSE BAG MROY TSE YOUNTAIN AND MROY DALE, AND BERE OM REAL DBARM-YAVE.
BSEN EKERG WUEHT SAD FEEN BELCOYED AND BAH MINALLG INHIDE TSE WATE, TSERE BERE HONWH, DANCEH, YUHIC, WAYEH, AND, OM COURHE, MOOD AND DRINV. TSERE BERE TSREE OMMICIAL YEALH: LUNCS, TEA, AND DINNER (OR HUPPER). FUT LUNCS AND TEA BERE YARVED CSIEMLG FG TSE MACT TSAT AT TSOHE TIYEH ALL TSE WUEHTH BERE HITTINW DOBN AND EATINW TOWETSER. FILFO MLUHSED, AND TSERE BAH AN ANWRG LIWST IN SIH EGEH. SIH VINDLG MACE WREB SARD. ‘BSG NOT?’ SE CRIED. ‘AND BSAT FUHINEHH IH IT OM GOURH, ANGBAG, TO VNOB BSAT I DO BITS YG OBN TSINWH? IT IH YG OBN. I MOUND IT. IT CAYE TO YE.’
‘GEH, GEH,’ HAID WANDALM. ‘FUT TSERE IH NO NEED TO WET ANWRG.’
‘IM I AY IT IH GOUR MAULT,’ HAID FILFO. ‘IT IH YINE, I TELL GOU. YG OBN. YG PRECIOUH. GEH, YG PRECIOUH.’
TSE BIZARD’H MACE REYAINED WRAKE AND ATTENTIKE, AND ONLG A MLICVER IN SIH DEEP EGEH HSOBED TSAT SE BAH HTARTLED AND INDEED ALARYED. ‘IT SAH FEEN CALLED TSAT FEMORE,’ SE HAID, ‘FUT NOT FG GOU.’
‘FUT I HAG IT NOB. AND BSG NOT? EKEN IM WOLLUY HAID TSE HAYE ONCE. IT’H NOT SIH NOB, FUT YINE. AND I HSALL VEEP IT, I HAG.’
WANDALM HTOOD UP. SE HPOVE HTERNLG. ‘GOU BILL FE A MOOL IM GOU DO. FILFO,’ SE HAID. ‘GOU YAVE TSAT CLEARER BITS EKERG BORD GOU HAG. IT SAH WOT MAR TOO YUCS SOLD ON GOU. LET IT WO! AND TSEN GOU CAN WO GOURHELM, AND FE MREE.’
‘I’LL DO AH I CSOOHE AND WO AH I PLEAHE,’ HAID FILFO OFHTINATELG.
‘NOB, NOB, YG DEAR SOFFIT! ‘ HAID WANDALM. ‘ALL GOUR LONW LIME BE SAKE FEEN MRIENDH, AND GOU OBE YE HOYETSINW. COYE! DO AH GOU PROYIHED: WIKE IT UP! ‘
‘BELL, IM GOU BANT YG RINW GOURHELM, HAG HO!’ CRIED FILFO. ‘FUT GOU BON’T WET IT. I BON’T WIKE YG PRECIOUH ABAG, I TELL GOU.’ SIH SAND HTRAGED TO TSE SILT OM SIH HYALL HBORD.
WANDALM’H EGEH MLAHSED. IT BILL FE YG TURN TO WET ANWRG HOON,’ SE HAID. IM GOU HAG TSAT AWAIN, I HSALL. TSEN GOU BILL HEE WANDALM TSE WREG UNCLOAVED.’ SE TOOV A HTEP TOBARDH TSE SOFFIT, AND SE HEEYED TO WROB TALL AND YENACINW; SIH HSADOB MILLED TSE LITTLE ROOY.
FILFO FACVED ABAG TO TSE BALL, FREATSINW SARD, SIH SAND CLUTCSINW AT SIH POCVET. TSEG HTOOD MOR A BSILE MACINW ONE ANOTSER, AND TSE AIR OM TSE ROOY TINWLED. WANDALM’H EGEH REYAINED FENT ON TSE SOFFIT. HLOBLG SIH SANDH RELAQED, AND SE FEWAN TO TREYFLE.
‘I DON’T VNOB BSAT SAH COYE OKER GOU, WANDALM,’ SE HAID. ‘GOU SAKE NEKER FEEN LIVE TSIH FEMORE. BSAT IH IT ALL AFOUT? IT IH YINE IHN’T IT? I MOUND IT, AND WOLLUY BOULD SAKE VILLED YE, IM I SADN’T VEPT IT. I’Y NOT A TSIEM, BSATEKER SE HAID.’
‘I SAKE NEKER CALLED GOU ONE,’ WANDALM ANHBERED. ‘AND I AY NOT ONE EITSER. I AY NOT TRGINW TO ROF GOU, FUT TO SELP GOU. I BIHS GOU BOULD TRUHT YE, AH GOU UHED.’ SE TURNED ABAG, AND TSE HSADOB PAHHED. SE HEEYED TO DBINDLE AWAIN TO AN OLD WREG YAN, FENT AND TROUFLED.
FILFO DREB SIH SAND OKER SIH EGEH. I AY HORRG,’ SE HAID. ‘FUT I MELT HO JUEER. AND GET IT BOULD FE A RELIEM IN A BAG NOT TO FE FOTSERED BITS IT ANG YORE. IT SAH FEEN HO WROBINW ON YG YIND LATELG. HOYETIYEH I SAKE MELT IT BAH LIVE AN EGE LOOVINW AT YE. AND I AY ALBAGH BANTINW TO PUT IT ON AND DIHAPPEAR, DON’T GOU VNOB; OR BONDERINW IM IT IH HAME, AND PULLINW IT OUT TO YAVE HURE. I TRIED LOCVINW IT UP, FUT I MOUND I COULDN’T REHT BITSOUT IT IN YG POCVET. I DON’T VNOB BSG. AND I DON’T HEEY AFLE TO YAVE UP YG YIND.’
‘TSEN TRUHT YINE,’ HAID WANDALM. ‘IT IH JUITE YADE UP. WO ABAG AND LEAKE IT FESIND. HTOP POHHEHHINW IT. WIKE IT TO MRODO, AND I BILL LOOV AMTER SIY.’
FILFO HTOOD MOR A YOYENT TENHE AND UNDECIDED. PREHENTLG SE HIWSED. ‘ALL RIWST,’ SE HAID BITS AN EMMORT. I BILL.’ TSEN SE HSRUWWED SIH HSOULDERH, AND HYILED RATSER RUEMULLG. ‘AMTER ALL TSAT’H BSAT TSIH PARTG FUHINEHH BAH ALL AFOUT, REALLG: TO WIKE ABAG LOTH OM FIRTSDAG PREHENTH, AND HOYESOB YAVE IT EAHIER TO WIKE IT ABAG AT TSE HAYE TIYE. IT SAHN’T YADE IT ANG EAHIER IN TSE END, FUT IT BOULD FE A PITG TO BAHTE ALL YG PREPARATIONH. IT BOULD JUITE HPOIL TSE XOVE.’
‘INDEED IT BOULD TAVE ABAG TSE ONLG POINT I EKER HAB IN TSE AMMAIR,’ HAID WANDALM.
‘KERG BELL,’ HAID FILFO, ‘IT WOEH TO MRODO BITS ALL TSE REHT.’ SE DREB A DEEP FREATS. ‘AND NOB I REALLG YUHT FE HTARTINW, OR HOYEFODG ELHE BILL CATCS YE. I SAKE HAID WOOD-FGE, AND I COULDN’T FEAR TO DO IT ALL OKER AWAIN.’ SE PICVED UP SIH FAW AND YOKED TO TSE DOOR.
‘GOU SAKE HTILL WOT TSE RINW IN GOUR POCVET,’ HAID TSE BIZARD. ‘BELL, HO I SAKE!’ CRIED FILFO. ‘AND YG BILL AND ALL TSE OTSER DOCUYENTH TOO. GOU SAD FETTER TAVE IT AND DELIKER IT MOR YE. TSAT BILL FE HAMEHT.’
‘NO, DON’T WIKE TSE RINW TO YE,’ HAID WANDALM. ‘PUT IT ON TSE YANTELPIECE. IT BILL FE HAME ENOUWS TSERE, TILL MRODO COYEH. I HSALL BAIT MOR SIY.’
FILFO TOOV OUT TSE ENKELOPE, FUT XUHT AH SE BAH AFOUT TO HET IT FG TSE CLOCV, SIH SAND XERVED FACV, AND TSE PACVET MELL ON TSE MLOOR. FEMORE SE COULD PICV IT UP, TSE BIZARD HTOOPED AND HEIZED IT AND HET IT IN ITH PLACE. A HPAHY OM ANWER PAHHED HBIMTLG OKER TSE SOFFIT’H MACE AWAIN. HUDDENLG IT WAKE BAG TO A LOOV OM RELIEM AND A LAUWS. ‘BELL, TSAT’H TSAT,’ SE HAID. ‘NOB I’Y OMM!’
TSEG BENT OUT INTO TSE SALL. FILFO CSOHE SIH MAKOURITE HTICV MROY TSE HTAND; TSEN SE BSIHTLED. TSREE DBARKEH CAYE OUT OM DIMMERENT ROOYH BSERE TSEG SAD FEEN FUHG.
‘IH EKERGTSINW READG?’ AHVED FILFO. ‘EKERGTSINW PACVED AND LAFELLED?’
‘EKERGTSINW,’ TSEG ANHBERED.
‘BELL, LET’H HTART TSEN!’ SE HTEPPED OUT OM TSE MRONT-DOOR.
IT BAH A MINE NIWST, AND TSE FLACV HVG BAH DOTTED BITS HTARH. SE LOOVED UP, HNIMMINW TSE AIR. ‘BSAT MUN! BSAT MUN TO FE OMM AWAIN, OMM ON TSE ROAD BITS DBARKEH! TSIH IH BSAT I SAKE REALLG FEEN LONWINW MOR, MOR GEARH! WOOD-FGE! ‘ SE HAID, LOOVINW AT SIH OLD SOYE AND FOBINW TO TSE DOOR. ‘WOOD-FGE, WANDALM!’
‘WOOD-FGE, MOR TSE PREHENT, FILFO. TAVE CARE OM GOURHELM! GOU ARE OLD ENOUWS, AND PERSAPH BIHE ENOUWS.’
‘TAVE CARE! I DON’T CARE. DON’T GOU BORRG AFOUT YE! I AY AH SAPPG NOB AH I SAKE EKER FEEN, AND TSAT IH HAGINW A WREAT DEAL. FUT TSE TIYE SAH COYE. I AY FEINW HBEPT OMM YG MEET AT LAHT,’ SE ADDED, AND TSEN IN A LOB KOICE, AH IM TO SIYHELM, SE HANW HOMTLG IN TSE DARV:
TSE ROAD WOEH EKER ON AND ON
DOBN MROY TSE DOOR BSERE IT FEWAN.
NOB MAR ASEAD TSE ROAD SAH WONE,
AND I YUHT MOLLOB, IM I CAN,
PURHUINW IT BITS EAWER MEET,
UNTIL IT XOINH HOYE LARWER BAG
BSERE YANG PATSH AND ERRANDH YEET.
AND BSITSER TSEN? I CANNOT HAG.
